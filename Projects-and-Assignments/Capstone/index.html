Magic Cube

Magic The Gathering is a trading card game where players construct decks to battle it out amongst each other. While normally you would construct these decks by buying individual packs of cards, a magic cube allows you to use prebuilt packs over and over again without the need to purchase more. This Magic cube set seeks to go a step further and create completely new custom cards and add completely new rules.

  • 560+ cards
  • 6 mana colors giving 21 pairings
  • 15 new keywords and abilities

Play with creatures belonging to 4 factions

Draft special cards that can give instant synergies

Adapt your commander in unique ways

Complete achievements for bragging rights
